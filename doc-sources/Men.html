About me

I work writing free software for SUSE, mainly on the infrastructure for the GNOME Project, which I co-founded.

I live in Mexico with my lovely wife Oralia Silva-Rueda. She took this nice picture of me near the bathrooms of the Tate Modern gallery in London back in 2003.
